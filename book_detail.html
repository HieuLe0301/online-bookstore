Diary of a Wimpy Kid

This is a book about a kid named Greg Heffley, as well as his hilarious everyday life entailing his one-of-the-kind parents and friends.

Through a very wonderful adventure, he soon realizes that even though his mom and dad could be annoying at times, his friend could be acting up in a silly way now and then, and things might went wrong with his life, those people will never leave him behind. Except for his punky brother Rodrick.

Dive right in to the colorful and exhilirating world of Diary of a Wimpy Kid and enjoy the journey!!
